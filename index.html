Frequently Asked Questions

Q. How do I apply?

Get in touch via the contact form

Q. Can I park my car?

Absolutely! We have two parking spots. First park in front of the office (number 217), come in to collect the parking card and then park in the parking "Amerika", just 50 meters away.

Q. How can I make a change to my application?

We have a 2-months cancellation policy.

Q. What are entry requirements?

None. We prioritize applications from freelancers and cloud native engineers, but our doors are open to everyone

Q. Can I host my event at Cloud Pirates?

We are delighted to host any kind of event at the office. We have full facilities to record and stream professionally, 80+ chairs, 150" projector, digital whiteboard and 6 wireless microphones. We'd be happy to support you and your community or company and promote your events through our extensive network.
